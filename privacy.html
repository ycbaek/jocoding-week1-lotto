Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Daily Toolkit. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our services:

  • Review Form: When you submit a review, we collect your name, email address, rating, and message. This data is processed through Formspree, a third-party form handling service.
  • Webcam Data: The Dog or Cat AI classifier accesses your webcam temporarily. Images are processed locally in your browser using TensorFlow.js and are never uploaded to any server.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage data through cookies and third-party analytics to understand how visitors use our site.

Cookies and Advertising

This website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites on the internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on visits to this site and/or other sites on the internet.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements. Google Privacy Policy
  • Formspree: For processing review form submissions. Formspree Privacy Policy
  • Google Teachable Machine / TensorFlow.js: For the AI image classifier. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Data Retention

Review submissions are retained by Formspree according to their data retention policies. Webcam data is not stored. We do not maintain a separate database of user information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Withdraw consent for webcam access at any time through your browser settings

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through our contact form.
