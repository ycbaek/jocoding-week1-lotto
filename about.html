About Daily Toolkit

What is Daily Toolkit?

Daily Toolkit is a free collection of fun and practical web-based tools designed to make your everyday decisions a little easier and a lot more enjoyable. Whether you need a lucky set of lotto numbers, a quick lunch suggestion, or want to test an AI model that tells dogs from cats, we have something for you.

Our Tools

Lotto Number Generator

Our lotto number generator uses a random algorithm to produce two unique groups of numbers. Each group consists of 5 regular numbers drawn from 1 to 49 and 1 mega ball drawn from 1 to 25. The numbers are color-coded by range so you can quickly scan your picks. It's a fun way to choose your next set of lottery numbers without any bias.

Lunch Menu Recommender

Decision fatigue is real, especially when it comes to lunch. Our lunch recommender randomly selects from a handpicked list of 15 popular dishes spanning Korean, Japanese, Italian, and American cuisines. From Kimchi Jjigae and Bibimbap to Pasta Carbonara and Beef Burgers, each recommendation comes with a description so you know exactly what you're getting into. Just click the button and let fate decide your next meal.

Dog or Cat AI Classifier

Powered by Google's Teachable Machine and TensorFlow.js, this tool uses your webcam to classify images as either a dog or a cat. The model was custom-trained to recognize the two animals. When you start the camera, a 3-second countdown gives you time to position your pet (or a photo of one) in front of the webcam. After the countdown, the AI analyzes the image and displays its prediction along with a confidence percentage. All processing happens locally in your browser — no images are uploaded anywhere.

Review & Feedback Form

We value your feedback. The review section lets you rate your experience with our tools on a 5-star scale and leave a written message. Your input helps us understand what's working well and what we can improve. All submissions are handled securely through Formspree.

Our Mission

We believe that simple tools can bring a lot of joy. Daily Toolkit was built with the goal of creating small, useful utilities that anyone can use for free, with no sign-ups or downloads required. We're constantly exploring new ideas and welcome your suggestions for tools you'd like to see added.

Technology

Daily Toolkit is built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no heavy frameworks, no unnecessary complexity. The AI classifier leverages TensorFlow.js for in-browser machine learning. We prioritize fast loading times, accessibility, and a clean user experience.
